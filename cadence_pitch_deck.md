## Slide 1: The Vision
**Brand Name:** Cadence (formerly Fluently)
**Mission:** To democratize high-quality speech therapy and communication coaching using state-of-the-art AI.

**The Elevator Pitch:**
Cadence is a multi-modal AI platform that helps individuals overcome stuttering, reduce regional accent interference (MTI), and improve word retrieval speed. We provide an "AI Lab" in your pocket that analyzes not just what you say, but how you move and sound.

## Slide 2: The Silent Barriers
**The Problem We're Solving:**
1. **The Cost Barrier:** Traditional speech therapy costs ₹1000–3000 per session, making it inaccessible for the masses.
2. **The Placement Barrier:** Millions of brilliant students from non-metro cities (Bihar, UP, Punjab, Tamil Nadu, etc.) face rejection in corporate placements due to "Mother Tongue Influence" (MTI) and accent barriers.
3. **The Confidence Gap:** Word retrieval issues ("tip-of-the-tongue" syndrome) and stammering cause social anxiety and limit professional growth.

*   **Multi-Modal Analysis:** We don't just listen; we watch. Analysis of facial tension, lip movement, and vocal patterns.
*   **Industry Standard Reporting:** We generate **AMCAT-Style Speech Reports**, the gold standard in corporate hiring, giving users a professional-grade audit of their speech.

---

## Slide 4: The Engine (Technological Edge)
**How the AI Models Work Together:**

### Phase 1: The Foundation (OpenAI Whisper)
*   **Role:** High-fidelity transcription and millisecond-accurate word timing.
*   **What it does:** Extracts exactly when every word was said. This allows Cadence to pinpoint the **exact word** where a stutter or block occurred by measuring unnatural silence intervals.

### Phase 2: The Specialist (Google Gemini 1.5 Flash)
*   **Role:** The "Expert Speech Pathologist."
*   **What it does:** Analyzes the timing and text from Whisper to identify:
    *   **Stuttering Patterns:** Identifying repetitions, prolongations, and fillers ("um", "uh").
    *   **MTI Patterns:** Detecting specific regional influences (e.g., v/w confusion).
    *   **Sentiment & Tone:** Evaluating the user's confidence and professional clarity.

---

## Slide 5: Key Features (The "Wow" Factor)
1.  **Word-Level Stutter Analysis:** A timeline of the transcript where specific "stuck" words are highlighted based on acoustic blocks.
2.  **AI Mother Tongue Analysis:** Detailed identification of regional accent "bleeding" into English.
3.  **Facial Tension Heatmaps:** Visualization of physical struggle (jaw clenching, lip tension) during speech, detected via computer vision.
4.  **Adaptive Lexical Training:** If you struggle to find a word, the AI builds a custom "Synonym Chain" exercise just for you.
